    During our second semester at MMI Montb√©liard, we worked on a project that allowed us to put all our acquired skills into practice. This project consisted of creating an innovative website on a topic we had chosen beforehand. With my two friends, we first brainstormed an innovative website concept, Artisae, which was approved by the judges. However, during the second phase, which was the website creation phase, we decided to choose another topic that better suited our skills and interests.

    So, we created Eskiss, a website that connects parents and artists to give a second life to children's drawings. The concept is simple: parents upload their children's drawings onto the platform and artists can then transform them into unique works of art. This project allowed us to develop our creativity, design development skills, and communication skills. We worked hard for several weeks to bring our project to life. We created an intuitive and user-friendly interface, allowing users to navigate the platform easily. We also implemented a secure payment system so that parents can purchase the art pieces created from their children's drawings.

    We faced challenges during the creation of this website, but we managed to overcome them through our teamwork and determination. We worked closely together to ensure the quality of the website and were proud of the final result. Our experience in creating a website with Eskiss was very enriching, and we learned a lot about website creation, project management, and communication. We are proud of the work we accomplished, and we believe that this website can make a difference for parents and artists by offering them a new way to share their passion for art.
