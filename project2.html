    3F is a fictive clothing brand that I imagined and did all the branding around.

    Our goal was to create a high-quality whisky brand that is environmentally respectful. We started by brainstorming a name for the brand. After a series of brainstorming sessions, we chose a name that symbolizes our commitment to the quality of our product, respect for our values, and remaining true to ourselves: Elias & Paul. Next, we worked on the design of our whisky bottle, ensuring it was in harmony with our name and design values. We opted for a clean and modern design, with our monogram serving as the logo. We also ensured that, in the future development of the brand, our bottle would be made from recyclable materials to minimize its environmental impact. Finally, we created a mock-up to visualize our final product and highlight its unique benefits. We presented our environmentally responsible whisky brand to our professors and colleagues, and we were thrilled to see their enthusiasm for our project.

    This responsible whisky brand creation project represents much more than a simple educational exercise for us. It demonstrates our ability to reconcile our technical skills in marketing, design, and project management with our values. We are confident that our brand will be widely embraced by consumers because it combines quality with environmental respect. We are proud of our work and our commitment to sustainability and environmental protection. This project allowed us to acquire valuable skills in marketing, design, and project management, while also giving us the opportunity to contribute to a more sustainable future for all. We look forward to seeing our environmentally responsible whisky brand in the market, and we hope it will inspire others to engage in similar initiatives.
