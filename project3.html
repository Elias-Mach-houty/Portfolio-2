    of famous manga character

    Drawing is a passion that has taken a significant place in the lives of many artists. For some, it is a way to escape the daily grind, while for others, it is a way to express their creativity and imagination. For me, drawing has become much more than just a passion. It is a means of expressing myself and letting my creativity flow.

    I discovered drawing during the lockdown when I started looking for an activity to occupy my free time. At first, my drawings were far from perfect, but I persevered because I loved the idea of creating something with my own hands. I mainly drew manga characters because I really like that universe. Over time, I began to improve and enhance my level. I started enjoying drawing more and more and began spending more time on it. I quickly realized that drawing was much more than just a hobby for me, it had become a true passion. That's when I decided to invest in a graphics tablet so that I could draw directly on my computer.

    I found this method of drawing very enjoyable and intuitive, and I was able to progress quickly thanks to the tools available on the tablet. Drawing on a graphics tablet has become my favorite method because it allows me to experiment and try new things more easily than with just a pencil and paper. Today, I am proud of my drawings and my level. Of course, there is still much to learn and perfect, but I am happy to see all the progress I have made since my beginnings. Drawing has become an integral part of my life, an endless source of pleasure and creativity. I am convinced that this passion will never leave me, and that I will continue to draw for a long time.
